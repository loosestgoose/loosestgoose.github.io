About Me

I'm originally from the California Bay Area, though I'm currently living in Cleveland, Ohio to pursue higher education.

Throughout high school, I was heavily involved in school events, my high school robotics team, and perhaps surprisingly, my school theater department (maybe not so unsurprisingly, I was part of stage tech, and not part of the cast).

In college, I've dedicated my time to working on the Case Western Reserve University Motorsports team, which competes in the Baja SAE collegiate racing series. Check out my Resume or Portfolio for more details on my involvement there.
